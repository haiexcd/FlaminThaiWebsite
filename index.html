About Us

Our Story

Inspired by Sonh Phommavanh and Viengxay Sisomphan, Flamin Thai was founded to bring family and friends together to enjoy a unique experience around the dinner table.

Our Mission

At Flamin' Thai, our mission is to bring people together through the vibrant flavors of Thai cuisine. Every dish we serve is a celebration of tradition, crafted with fresh ingredients, bold spices, and a passion for excellence. Whether you're here for a quick bite or a special occasion, we aim to create an unforgettable dining experience that feels like home.

Catering
